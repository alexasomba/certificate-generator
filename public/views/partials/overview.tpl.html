Features

1
Bulk Mailer
You can send certificates in bulk by uploading an excel, txt or a csv file containing the information of users you want to send certificates to see instructions.
2
Send individual mails
You can send a certificate to a recepient just like you would an email by entering the details of the recepient and the type of certificate you want to send them. see instructions.
3
Certificate template builder
Generate a certificate template from any image template of your choice see instructions.
